About Us

Formed in 2007, Anakh-E-Gabroo (AEG) is an independent bhangra team representing the tri-state area. AEG has consistently placed at nationally ranked competitions and stands as one of the top Bhangra teams. Over the years, we have had the privilege of sharing the art of Bhangra with all kinds of people from all around the world. Even as our team grows, our five core values have stayed the same. They are, to be Impactful, to be Bold, to be Inclusive, to be Determined and to build Camaraderie.

F.A.Q

  • Do you perform outside of NY?

    Yes, we are available in the tri-state area (PA, NJ, NY).

  • Is anyone able to join the online/in person classes?

    Yes, anyone and everyone is welcom to join!

  • How much are the lessons?

    Our classes will usually be $15 per person unless stated otherwise. Check out the event flyer on instagram for further details.

  • Are you able to do multiple performances at our event?

    We are willing to work with you to make your event the way you want it. Pricing will reflect individual dancer compensation, total time dancing and travel costs.

  • What do you wear for performances?

    We wear Vaardi, turban and jewlery (traditional Bhangra attire) for all of our performances.

  • Do you offer dhol services?

    Yes, we can offer dhol services for an additional charge.
